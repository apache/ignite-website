This blog post explains why Yahoo! Japan chose Apache Ignite for a highly scalable caching system that can process tens of thousands of requests per second.

SQL queries were performed via thin clients since they do not require a standard topology join and therefore are easier to be handled through container services. After a successful POC, Ignite fully met their requirements and was quickly rolled into production.
